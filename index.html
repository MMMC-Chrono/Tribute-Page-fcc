"What is important is to know fear and yet take a step forward."

"I have to do what I can, while I can still do it, so... I'll be kicking and screaming, till the very end."

Here's the time line of Rosette Christopher's life:

age 6
Rosette and her younger brother, Joshua, were sent to the Seventh Bell Orphanage after their parents' death.
age 7
She became protective of Joshua who was very sick due to the strain of his Apostle power and immediately disliked Father Ewan Remington who came to visit Joshua. So, Rosette always kidnapped Joshua and took him into a nearby woods to avoid Father Remington.
age 9
Once, while hiding from Father Remington, both slipped and fell into a weak spot in the ground and accidently found pale and weak demon, Chrono who lost his horns while fighting with his twin brother Aion.
age 12
- As Joshua was kidnapped, brainwashed and given Chrono's horn, which made his sickness go away instinctly, by Aion, Rosette made a contract with Chrono to save Joshua, making her life slowly tick away.
- Since then Rosette became an exorcist and joined the special division called "The Order of Magdalene" to stop demons from causing chaos and to find her little brother Joshua.
age 13-15
She encountered several fights together with Chrono, Azmaria (the Apostle of Charity) who admired Rosette a lot and Stella (a Jewel Witch) who was finding her older sister kidnapped by Aion.
age 16
- As Stigmata appeared on Rosette's wrists and forehead, everyone found out that Rosette was "Mary of Magdalene", an apostle that ruled over all the rest and acquired power to see the past, present, future as well as being able to transform into an angel.
- The four had their last fight with Aion. During that, Stella died while fighting with her older sister brainwashed by Aion.
- A few days after the battle, Azmaria found Rosette and Chrono dead in a cottage.
